Research Assistant

  • National Health Research Institutes (NHRI), Taiwan
    Geriatric Center

  • Location
    Huwei, Yunlin. [National Taiwan University- Yunlin Campus]

  • Job Description
    Dr. Fanglin Kuo's project at NHRI is seeking 1 -2 research assistants at all levels. We conduct research in diverse areas of geriatrics, community care and functioning in older adults, including cognitive and mobility maintenance, optimal care activity design, population health in community-dwelling older adults, using measurements and interviews. Group members will gain experience in learning domain knowledge, performing scientific reasoning, writing programs, collecting and analyzing data, and interacting closely with domestic collaborators. We look for motivated persons curious about healthcare problems and willing to dedicate energy and time to solve them. Specifically, qualified candidates need to have background in either quantitative or quality analysis experience and basic analysis skills, and are anticipated to work in a collaborative environment. Applicants should send curriculum vitae and a brief statement of background and research interest to flkuo@nhri.edu.tw.

  • Salary
    Follow NHRI guideline. Research assistant with BS degree: NT$34,356/month or above, with Master degree: NT$40,245/month or above. Postdoctoral researcher: NT$57,914/month or above.
